QA operating style

A practical workflow for product quality, AI validation, and release readiness.

01

Plan risk

Review product scope, acceptance criteria, designs, edge cases, integrations, platform matrix, and release risk before execution.

02

Design coverage

Create manual scenarios across positive, negative, boundary, equivalence, E2E, mobile, localization, API, payment, and AI output flows.

03

Execute deeply

Run functional, regression, smoke, sanity, exploratory, cross-browser, Android, iOS, UAT, security, and DevTools-backed checks.

04

Validate AI

Test prompt adherence, hallucination risk, output consistency, fallback behavior, adversarial and injection prompts, and downstream business logic impact.

05

Sign off

Report defects clearly, retest fixes, summarize readiness, document known risks, and own release sign-off with full context.
